About Me


Hello! Originaly from Islamabad, Pakistan I was 6 years old when my family moved to Ottawa, Canada. Ever since I can remember I have always loved technology. Growing up I always wanted to work in tech. but I never knew exactly what career to pursue. Well, that was the case until I got to grade 10. In grade 10 I took a computer science class and absolutely fell in love with programming. From that point on I knew that I want to have a career as a software engineer.




Currently, I am a fourth-year computer science student at Carleton University.
The languages I love to program in are: JavaScript, Java, C, and SQL.
I have experience with technologies and frameworks such as HTML, CSS, Node, MongoDB, Linux, BASH, Angular, and Spring Boot.




I have completed 2 internships during my time at university. I worked as a developer for the Government of Canada - IRCC (Summer 2022) and more recently I worked as a full-stack developer at Innovapost (Summer 2023). I am currently looking for internships/new-grad roles. My expected graduation date is June 2024.
